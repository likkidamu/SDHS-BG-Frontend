Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Sri Datta Human Services (SDHS) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the SDHS BG Program mobile application ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate the App:

  • Volunteer ID and Password — used solely to authenticate you into the App.
  • Name and Role — received from our server after login to personalize your experience.
  • Session Token (JWT) — stored securely on your device using encrypted storage to keep you logged in.

We do not collect:

  • Location data
  • Contacts or camera access
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Any data from minors under 13

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate and maintain your session in the App
  • To display your schedule, attendance, grades, and assignments
  • To allow teachers and admins to manage program data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Your session token is stored using encrypted secure storage on your device (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore).
  • All communication between the App and our server is transmitted over HTTPS.
  • Your password is never stored on your device — only the session token after a successful login.
  • Our backend is hosted on Render, a secure cloud platform.

4. Data Retention

Session data stored on your device is automatically removed when you log out. Account data on our server is retained for the duration of your participation in the SDHS BG Program and removed upon request.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party infrastructure:

  • Render (render.com) — backend server hosting. Subject to Render's privacy policy.

No third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools are used.

6. Children's Privacy

The SDHS BG Program is intended for volunteers and participants aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out at any time by logging out of the App

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
